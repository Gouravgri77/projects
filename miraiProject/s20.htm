Admission Process

Be in the top 20% for our exclusive cohort.

  • Apply Online
    Register and fill out an online application form.
  • Take the MAINS test
    Take the Mirai National Screening (MAINS).
  • Appear for Personal Interview
    Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview with industry experts.
  • Receive your Offer Letter
    Offer Letters will be sent to selected candidates.
  • Block your seat
    Admission is confirmed upon submission of required fees and documents.

Important Information

•⁠ Hostel facilities at Mirai School of Technology (Hi-Tech Institute) include both AC and non-AC room options. Charges cover accommodation and basic amenities. Rooms are allotted on a first-come, first-served basis.


•⁠ At Mirai School of Technology (Rathinam Institute), on-campus accommodation is available for girls. For boys, the hostel is located near the campus with pick-up and drop facilities provided.


•⁠  ⁠The fee mentioned above covers only academic tuition and Mirai School of Technology charges. Additional expenses, such as hostel, mess, university registration, and examination fees, will be payable separately by the student as per actual costs.
