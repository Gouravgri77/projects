Important Information

• Hostel facilities at Mirai School of Technology (Hi-Tech Institute) include both AC and non-AC room options.
Charges cover accommodation and basic amenities. Rooms are allotted on a first-come, first-served basis.

•⁠ At Mirai School of Technology (Rathinam Institute), on-campus accommodation is available for girls. For boys, the
